Common Questions

How do I get started with vakaflow?

vakaflow is designed to help you make informed options trading decisions. After signing in with Apple ID or Google, you can start by:

  • Setting up your investor profile (risk tolerance, experience level)
  • Exploring stock recommendations in the Recommendation Inbox
  • Tracking your portfolio and options positions
  • Joining vakacircles to connect with other traders

How do recommendations work?

vakaflow uses AI-powered analysis to provide trading recommendations. Recommendations are based on:

  • Your investor profile and risk tolerance
  • Real-time market data from Yahoo Finance
  • AI analysis from ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude
  • Historical performance tracking

Is my data secure?

Yes! vakaflow stores most data locally on your device. We use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I sync data across devices?

vakaflow primarily stores data locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, some data may be synced via Apple's CloudKit service. You control this through your device settings.

What if I have a bug or feature request?

Please email us at support@vakaflow.app with:

  • A description of the issue or feature
  • Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
  • Your device and iOS version
  • App version (found in Settings)
