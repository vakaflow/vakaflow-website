3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: Most of your data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • Device-Specific: Data is tied to your specific device and is not automatically synced across devices
  • Encryption: Sensitive data (when stored in Keychain) is encrypted using iOS security features

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • CloudKit: If you enable iCloud sync, some data may be synced to Apple's CloudKit service
    • CloudKit data is encrypted in transit and at rest
    • You control which data is synced via iCloud settings
    • Apple's CloudKit Privacy Policy applies to synced data

3.3 Security Measures

  • Authentication: Secure sign-in via Apple ID or Google Sign-In (industry-standard OAuth)
  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data encrypted at rest (Keychain) and in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access Controls: User data is isolated and accessible only by you (except shared vakacircle data)
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update the App to address security vulnerabilities

3.4 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal data from local storage
  • Backup Data: If you use iCloud backup, your App data may be included in your device backup (subject to Apple's privacy policies)

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

  • AI Services: When you request AI analysis, we send your query (which may include stock symbols or trading strategies) to the AI service you selected. We do not send your personal financial data unless you explicitly include it in your query.

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users
